📚 Building My Personal Brand

I’m structuring my public work around three channels, each with a distinct purpose and cadence:

1. Weekly Articles

Long-form writing published once a week. This is where I explore ideas in depth—engineering, tools, productivity, world-building, or anything I’m actively learning. Each article aims to provide clarity, structure, and something actionable.

2. Informal Logs

Short, frequent updates. These are lighter notes: experiments, progress snapshots, frustrations, and small discoveries. Not daily, but more frequent than the weekly articles. They serve as a running record of what I’m building and thinking about.

You can also check my 🐤 X for daily updates.

3. Monthly YouTube Videos

One video each month. These will be broader or more polished: breakdowns, walkthroughs, dev logs, tech overviews, or behind-the-scenes looks at my projects. Video lets me communicate tone and context that writing can’t.
